  • Masymanwiki in nutshell.

    Masymanwiki is a free online guide book that helps people learn to do things like cooking and expelling wasps. Masymanwiki is owned by Maamedia, which was developed for this purpose, which is neither a company nor an organization. Masymanwiki is written in different languages and language wikis are constantly being developed. Masymanwiki is a wiki site and a site like Wikipedia in appearance and settings, but with a completely different content. The purpose of Masymanwiki is completely different from Wikipedia. Masymanwiki is a multilingual guide book, while Wikipedia is an encyclopedia. However, Masymanwiki has sister wikis that resemble Wikimedia wikis, such as the Maamedia Commons-Wiki (which is the repository for images and logos of both Masymanwiki and Maedia) or the Data-Wiki (a large knowledge database).
    Masymanwiki was founded as early as December 2020, when its purpose was to make Wikipedia editors easier. However, such a wiki was renamed and Masymanwiki was re-established in late January 2021 and then became the purpose of a "guide book". Masymanwiki is completely free and ad-free and can be edited by anyone. Like Wikipedia, Masymanwiki has rules to follow. Updates and settings changes in Masymanwiki are the responsibility of the founders and software coders, so ordinary editors and readers do not have to worry about keeping them up to date. Masymanwiki strives to always have the latest version: this is still the case today.
